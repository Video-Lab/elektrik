About Us

Our Core Values

Eco-Friendliness

Our cars are environmentally friendly- running primarily on electricity & using recycled materials are only two of the many environmental benefits of ELEKTRIK-brand cars.

Performance

Our cars are lightweight, powerful, and capable of high speeds, giving you a range of cars that can suit your needs.

Longevity

With an average lifespan of 10-15 years, you won't need to replace your ELEKTRIK car anytime soon.

Our History

ELEKTRIK was originally created as an auto repair shop in 2020 in Katy, Texas by Jad Khalili and Lakshay Gupta. They offered car maintenance & repair services, and eventually ventured into extending their scope to electric vehicles. As more shops opened across the country, we decided to start converting our repair shops into manufacturing plants designed to produce a new line of high-performance, environmentally friendly electric cars. As of today, we manufacture 3 main car models & have over 100 ELEKTRIK dealerships open in North America.

Our Mission

Unlike other electric car companies, we are not exclusively focused on bringing about a new frontier in automobile technology; we are also focused on coupling innovation with the same comfort & performance as any other car. We have proven to match up to any other automobile brands on the market; electric or otherwise.
